Features

Key features of SelCont include:

  • A user-friendly environment for the instructor, the operational support team and the end-user (e.g. student). The toolkit should not require extensive post-processing and skilled support personnel. The instructor should face minimal involvement in post-processing and the support team should be supplied with all necessary synchronization data by the instructor’s device (a tablet or laptop).
  • Support features for the instructor’s device, such as document and slide presentations, stylus-based annotations and white-board notes, web browsing. These will be combined with audio/video recording at the post processing stage, based on data – tags annotated in real time, as the lecture proceeds.
  • Minimal post-processing, that will not affect the course material but will produce the synchronized content and will cater for its Internet delivery to end-users (e.g. students). The latter can rely on cloud-based Video on Demand services.
  • Ease to install in any lecture room or auditorium. Projection, video and audio capturing equipment could be pre-installed or portable as needed.

SeLCont will take advantage from the most recent advances in Internet technologies and aspires to fill the gap for user-friendly, scalable and labor efficient tools for production and delivery of on-line courses.
